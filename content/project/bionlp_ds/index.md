1) Proposed a new large-scale dataset, BioRel, for distant supervised relation extraction in biomedical domain. The dataset is created by aligning UMLS relations to Metamapped Medline Corpus.
2) Conducted comprehensive experiments to show that the dataset is suitable for the state-of-the art neural-based methods compared to traditional statistic methods. Further analysis also suggested the dataset have fewer noisy instances.
